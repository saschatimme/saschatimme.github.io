Software Projects

  • HomotopyContinuation.jl: A Julia package for the numerical solution of polynomial systems by homotopy continuation methods.
  • Polymake.jl: A Julia interface to polymake.
  • MixedSubdivisions.jl: A Julia package for computing a (fine) mixed subdivision and the mixed volume of lattice polytopes.
  • PolynomialAmoebas.jl: Visualization and computations related to Amoebas in 2 and 3 variables.
